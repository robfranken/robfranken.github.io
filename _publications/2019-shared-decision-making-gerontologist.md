Persons with dementia (PWDs) and their caregivers often face difficult decisions regarding housing and care relocation. We performed a qualitative data analysis to assess the extent to which these decisions for PWDs with their formal and informal caregivers correspond to an interprofessional shared decision making (IP-SDM) approach, and what light this approach sheds on their experiences with decision making.
