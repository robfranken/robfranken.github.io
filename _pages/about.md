I'm currently pursuing my PhD at Radboud University Nijmegen in the Department of Sociology. My research focuses on the drivers of social networks, perceptions in/of social networks, and network influence effects on health behavior and opinion dynamics.

For additional information, please explore my [publications](https://robfranken.github.io/publications/), access my [CV](https://robfranken.github.io/files/cv.pdf), and look forward to more updates in the future. You can easily navigate through the sections using the top menu on this website.
